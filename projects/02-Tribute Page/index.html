Michael Crichton

The man with a 1000 skills

Here is the timeline of Doctor Crichton

  • 1942 - Born in Chicago, Illinois
  • 1960 - Enter Harvard
  • 1965 - Wrote his first novel "Odds On"
  • 1969 - Graduate from Harvard Medical School and wrote his first bestsellers "The Andromeda Strain"
  • 1972 - Wrote "The Terminal Man"
  • 1973 - Wrote and directed "Westworld"
  • 1975 - Wrote "The Great Train Robbery"
  • 1978 - Directed "Coma" & "The Great Train Robbery"
  • 1980 - Wrote "Congo"
  • 1981 - Directed "Looker"
  • 1983 - Wrote "Electronic Life", a book that introduces BASIC programming
  • 1984 - Developed and released the graphical adventure video game "Amazon" (strongly based on "Congo")
  • 1984 - Directed "Runaway"
  • 1987 - Wrote "Sphere"
  • 1990 - Wrote "Jurassic Park"
  • 1992 - Wrote "Rising Sun"
  • 1992 - Ranked among People magazine's 50 most beautiful people
  • 1994 - Wrote "Disclosure"
  • 1994 - Created the TV serie "ER"
  • 1995 - Wrote "The Lost World"
  • 1995 - Awarded the "Academy Award for Technical Achievement" for pioneering use of computer programs in movies
  • 1996 - Wrote "Airframe"
  • 1999 - Wrote "Timeline"
  • 2002 - Wrote "Prey"
  • 2004 - Wrote "State of Fear"
  • 2006 - Wrote "Next"
  • 2008 - Diead of Lymphoma at the age of 66
